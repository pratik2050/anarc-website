About Us

This club provides an excellent platform for the students who want to plant their interests in the field of ROBOTICS and ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE. The robotics club at NIT Agartala is an interdisciplinary club whose gate is open for all who want to pursue their interests in the mechanical, electronics, automation, control, and software field. ANARC offers myriad opportunities to learn more about designing, construction, programming, and characterization of ROBOTICS and CONTROL SYSTEM throughout the year.
