ABOUT SOAR

SOAR-Student Orientation and Registration program.

It aims to introduce the freshers to our robotics club. The Orientation Program is addressed by Director and Faculty Advisor. The working body of ANARC present their several completed and ongoing projects. You are welcome to witness the glorious journey of our club.
